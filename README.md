# Source-of-Investment-Analysis
Key Insights
Financial Consultants are the most trusted source of investment information, accounting for 128 investors (40%).
Newspapers and Magazines are the second most preferred source, used by 112 investors (35%).
Television is a less common source, with 48 investors (15%) relying on it.
The Internet is the least preferred source in this dataset, used by 32 investors (10%).
Overall, 75% of investors rely on professional advice and print media, highlighting the importance of trusted and traditional information sources in investment decisions.
